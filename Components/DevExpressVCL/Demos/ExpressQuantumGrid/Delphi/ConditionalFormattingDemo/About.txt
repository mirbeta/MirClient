In this demo, you can:
- Invoke the grid's context menu and choose from the predefined rules, data bars, icons, and color scales and apply them to the focused column's cells displaying numeric values. 
- Click Options | Manage Rules... to display a built-in dialog that allows you to: 
  - Create rules and customize them and their appearance settings. 
  - Specify how rules apply to cells and values. 
 
- Clear rules applied to all or specific columns. 
- Apply different paint styles to a form (the Look&Feel menu item).

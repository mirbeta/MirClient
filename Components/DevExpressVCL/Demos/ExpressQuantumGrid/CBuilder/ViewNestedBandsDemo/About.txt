In this demo you can:

- use nested bands to display data (see Options/Nested Bands)

- quickly customize the visibility of bands by clicking on the indicator located to the left of band headers (see Options/Bands quick customization)

- quickly customize the visibility of columns by clicking on the indicator located to the left of column headers  (see Options/Columns quick customization)

- use drag&drop to move a column or band

- merge cells that contain the same data (see Options/Cell merging)

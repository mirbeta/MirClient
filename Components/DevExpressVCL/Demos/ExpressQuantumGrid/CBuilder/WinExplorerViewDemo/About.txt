This demo shows how to display records with images and short captions using the WinExplorer View.

In this demo, you can:
- Choose from the available display modes (Small images, Medium images, Large images, Extra large images, Tiles, List, or Content).
- Group records by a specific attribute and change their sort order.
- Experiment with the appearance and behavior options displayed above the grid.
- Apply different paint styles to a form (the Look&Feel menu item).

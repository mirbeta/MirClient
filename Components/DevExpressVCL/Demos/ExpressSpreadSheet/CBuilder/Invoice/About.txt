This demo shows how to create a simple invoice using the built-in spreadsheet capabilities.

In this demo, you can:
- Change the product quantity, price and discount values. All invoice values are recalculated automatically.
- See the formulas used for calculation (the Options | Show Formulas menu item).
- Manage the product list by adding/removing spreadsheet rows.
- Scroll the product list and see how the Frozen panes feature works.
- Apply different paint styles to a form (the Look&Feel menu item).

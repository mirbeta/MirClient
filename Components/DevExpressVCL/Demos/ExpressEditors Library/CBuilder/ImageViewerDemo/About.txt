In this demo you can perform the following operations with the film gallery:

- Group films by genre.
- Adjust the gallery item size.
- Customize gallery text and column options.
- Select a film to see its cover art image in the preview section.
- Size an application window or drag a splitter to see how the gallery arranges its contents based on specified column options.
- Switch the visibility of the preview section and specify its background color.
- Zoom the cover art image in the preview section.
- Change the cover art image size.

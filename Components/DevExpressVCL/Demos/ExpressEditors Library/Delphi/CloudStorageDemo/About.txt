This demo allows you to

 - Choose the online service you want to access and sign in to your account (Options | Cloud Storage Provider submenu):

    - Google Drive: Click Allow in the "Request for Permission" window to grant the demo access to the account's resources with the permissions listed in this window.

    - Microsoft OneDrive: Click Accept in the "Sign in to your account" window to grant the demo access to the account's resources with the permissions listed in this window.

 - Browse the folder hierarchy on the connected drive.

 - Create or delete folders.

 - Upload files.

 - Double-click a file to download and open it in an associated application.

Important: The demo does not store the client application's credentials (client ID or secret).

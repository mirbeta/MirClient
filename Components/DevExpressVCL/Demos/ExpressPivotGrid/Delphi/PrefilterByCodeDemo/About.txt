In this demo you can:

- Apply the predefined prefilter templates using the combo box.
- Click the "Custom prefilter" item to invoke the Filter Builder dialog to apply complex filter conditions.
- Use the prefilter panel's button to display the data unfiltered.
- Click the prefilter panel's button to clear the currently applied prefilter.
- Change the prefilter panel's position (the Options/Prefilter Position menu).
